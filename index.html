Unilever Canteen Ordering System

This was a unique project because Unilever is a British-Dutch FMCG company, headquartered in London, England. Unilever is one of the oldest FMCG companies, and its products are available in around 190 countries. This project objetive was to solve the issues employees experienced during lunch time in the canteen, and the employees requested a system that would permit a canteen user to order meals online, to be delivered to their work location at a specified time and date. The system we created enhanced employee satisfaction, improved employee productivity, reduced food wastage and inefficiencies in the canteen operation.
